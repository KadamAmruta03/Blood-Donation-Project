  • One blood donation can save up to 3 lives.
  • Blood cannot be manufactured; it comes only from donors.
  • Every 2 seconds, someone needs blood.
  • Only about 3% of eligible donors donate regularly.
  • Blood components (red cells, platelets, plasma) help different patients.
  • Blood donation is safe even if slightly anemic (after doctor approval).
  • Plasma helps burn victims and cancer patients.
  • Platelets help patients undergoing chemotherapy.
  • Blood donation improves community health awareness.

  • Have a light, healthy meal before donation.
